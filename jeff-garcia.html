Educational Journey

Jeff Garcia attended Gilroy High School which is located in Gilroy, California. At this school, this personality was a letterman in football and basketball.

As a senior at this school, this personality was a South Santa Clara County selection for the Charlie Wedemeyer All-Star Football Game.

As such, he played defensive back in the game. He graduated from Gilroy High School in the year 1988.

Following this, he attended Gavilan College and was the starting quarterback on the football team for the 1989 season.

He then earned junior college honorable mention All-America honors after the season.

In the year 1990, this personality transferred to San Jose State University. Then from 1991 to 1993, this athlete was the starting quarterback on the San Jose State Spartans football team.

Under this player, San Jose State went 6-4-1 in 1991, 7–4 in 1992, and 2–9 in 1993.

Also in 1991, this athlete had a career-high 61.9% completion rate at San Jose State. For his junior season in the year 1992, this athlete earned UPI All-America honors.

As of 2007, this personality had the most career offensive yards in San Jose State history, i.e. 7,274.
