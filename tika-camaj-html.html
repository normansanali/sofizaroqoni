Tika Camaj - Age, Family, Bio

Swimsuit and lingerie model best recognized for her appearances in ads for Victoria's Secret. Her versatile look and skills have earned her appearances in both print and runway campaigns.

Before Fame

In 2009, she was one of ten women to compete for a spot modeling on the Victoria's Secret runway on CBS.

Trivia

Surprisingly she is a skilled martial artist and has become a Taekwondo champion.

Family Life

She has a sister named Ariana. Her cousins Jehona and Aferdita Dreshaj are incredibly popular Instagram stars. She has been in a relationship with David Silverman.

Associated With

She has modeled alongside her friend Joy Corrigan and has posted photos with her to Instagram.
