How much are the Travis Scott Jordans Retail?

Travis Scott's collaboration with Jordan Brand has produced a series of highly sought-after sneakers, with the Air Jordan 1 Low collection being a standout example.

The partnership between the renowned rapper and the iconic sneaker brand has resulted in unique designs that have garnered significant attention in the sneaker world.

Travis Scott x Air Jordan 1 Low Collaboration

The Travis Scott x Air Jordan 1 Low collaboration is a testament to the creative genius of Travis Scott and the enduring appeal of the Air Jordan 1 silhouette.

This partnership between the rapper and Jordan Brand has resulted in the creation of unique and highly sought-after sneakers that have captured the attention of sneaker enthusiasts worldwide.

Retail Price and Availability

The retail price of the Travis Scott x Air Jordan 1 Low sneakers varies based on the size and style.

The retail prices typically cater to all, with adult pairs being priced at $150, preschool sizes at $60, and toddler options at $50 USD.

These sneakers are available at select Jordan Brand retailers, both in-store and online on Nike.com.

Due to the high demand and limited releases, securing a pair at retail price can be quite challenging, often leading to these sneakers being resold at significantly higher prices in the secondary market.

Travis Scott x Air Jordan 1 Low 'Black Olive'

One of the notable releases from this collaboration is the Travis Scott x Air Jordan 1 Low OG "Black Olive," which was set to release in Fall 2024.

This particular edition features a unique color palette of Black, Sail, Medium Olive, and Muslin, staying true to the collaborative efforts of Travis Scott and Jordan Brand.

The design elements include a mix of leather and nubuck, mesh tongues, and inner lining, with notable details such as the iconic backward Swoosh on the side and Cactus Jack tongue branding.

The release date for this edition was set for September 12, 2024, with prices ranging from $50 to $150, making it accessible to a wide range of fans.

Exclusive Designs and Limited Releases

The Travis Scott x Air Jordan 1 Low sneakers are known for their unique design elements, including the reverse Swoosh, premium materials, and distinct color palettes, making them highly coveted among sneaker enthusiasts.

The releases of these sneakers are typically limited, contributing to their exclusivity and high demand among collectors and fans of both Travis Scott and the Air Jordan brand.

Resale Market Dynamics

The high demand for the Travis Scott x Air Jordan 1 Low sneakers often leads to them being resold at prices significantly higher than their retail value.

The limited availability and the unique design elements of these sneakers contribute to their resale value, with prices often fluctuating based on market demand and the specific edition of the sneaker.

The resale prices for the early pairs of previous Travis Scott collaborations with Jordan Brand have seen significant fluctuations, with some pairs initially selling for high prices before experiencing a drop in the resale market.

 

Ultimately, the retail prices of the Travis Scott x Air Jordan 1 Low sneakers reflect the commitment of Jordan Brand to make these highly sought-after sneakers accessible to a wide range of fans.

The collaboration between Travis Scott and Jordan Brand has resulted in a series of unique and exclusive designs that have captured the attention of sneaker enthusiasts and collectors worldwide. The retail prices, combined with the limited releases, contribute to the desirability and high demand for these sneakers, making them a coveted addition to any sneaker collection.

From the distinct design elements to the limited availability, the Travis Scott x Air Jordan 1 Low sneakers continue to be a focal point of excitement within the sneaker community.
