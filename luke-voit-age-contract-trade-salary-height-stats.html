Luke Voit Age, Contract, Trade, Salary, Height, Stats

Baseball first baseman for the Washington Nationals, Luke Voit, is 31 years old. He was born on February 13, 1991, in Wildwood, Missouri, United States.

Luke Voit Contract

Before joining the Washington Nationals, Luke signed a one-year contract worth $5,450,000 with the San Diego Padres.

Luke Voit Trade

In March 2022, Luke was traded by the New York Yankees to the San Diego Padres for Justin Lange.

In August 2022, Luke was traded to the Washington Nationals for Juan Soto and Josh Bell.

Luke Voit Salary

Luke earned $5,450,000, as salary while he was with the San Diego Padres. However, his new pay with the Washington Nationals has been adjusted to $1,916,480.
