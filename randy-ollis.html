Randy Ollis Bio | Wiki

Randy Ollis is a popular American meteorologist. Currently, Randy serves as a meteorologist for Daybreak and the noon news at WISH-TV since 1984. Before, Randy worked as a meteorologist at KTVY-TV, Oklahoma City, to move to Indianapolis. Prior to that, Randy held positions at WDTN-TV, Dayton, Ohio, and WSAW-TV in Wausau, Wisconsin.
