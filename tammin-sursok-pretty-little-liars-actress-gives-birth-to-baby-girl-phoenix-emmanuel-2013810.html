Tammin Sursok Baby: Pretty Little Liars Star Gives Birth to Phoenix

Welcome to the world, baby girl! ABC Family star Tammin Sursok, who plays Jenna on the hit series Pretty Little Liars, gave birth to a daughter on Tuesday, Oct. 8, her rep tells Us Weekly exclusively.

"Phoenix Emmanuel Sursok-McEwen was born today," the rep tells Us. She weighed in at 9 pounds, 12 ounces.

This is the first child for Sursok, 30, and husband Sean McEwen, an actor/director. The couple originally confirmed her pregnancy to Us in June, saying they felt "very blessed to be having a baby" and would be "happy with either a boy or a girl." Two months later, however, the former Young and the Restless actress revealed exclusively to Us that it was the latter.

"I'm so excited to enter this new phase of our lives," the mom-to-be said at the time. "What a blessing to add a little girl to our family!"

Married since August 2011, the couple hope to add more little ones to their family soon. "We want like three or four," she said in an interview with Australian radio deejays Kyle and Jackie O earlier this year. "But a lot of Tammins running around — I don't know if the world needs anymore."
