Kylie Jenner's Black and White Chanel Outfits on Winter Vacation

  • Beauty mogul Kylie Jenner is enjoying the perfect winter vacation in the snow with family and friends.
  • On Instagram, she showed off some of her picture-perfect outfits.

Don't kid yourself—ski trips are definitely more about the ski outfits than the act of actually skiing. Yesterday, Kylie Jenner showed off her winter-wonderland-worthy outfits on Instagram. With the backdrop of snow-dusted trees or in the warmth of a wooden cabin, Jenner went from snow bunny to snow vixen in a matter of a few snaps.

For her first look, Jenner wore an all-white jumpsuit from Naked Wardrobe, while friend Yris Palmer color-coordinated in all-black. The friends also both wore Chanel mufflers and cuffs (both made of rabbit fur) from vintage retailer What Goes Around Comes Around, according to Page Six. The lapin mufflers retail at $2,500 and the lapin bracelets at $1,050 each.

Next up, Jenner showed off an all-black outfit that featured a ribbed turtleneck, high-waisted leather pants, Chanel earmuffs, and–the kicker–dramatic elbow-length, lambskin Chanel gloves. While the earmuffs sell for $1,250 on the luxury vintage retailer's website, the gloves are currently retailing for $765.

In the photo, Jenner leans against a frost-covered window while holding a glass of white wine.

Earlier today, the Kylie Cosmetics founder posted yet another luxe snow outfit. Jenner rocked the puff in an all Fendi snow suit, featuring a black fur-lined hood and Fendi logo stripes running down the length of her sleeves.
