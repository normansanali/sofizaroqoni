Wiki/Biography

Rrahul Narain Kanal was born on Friday, 27 September 1985 (age 37 years; as of 2022) in Mumbai, Maharashtra. He grew up in Bandra, Mumbai.

Rrahul Narain Kanal’s childhood picture

Rrahul studied at St. Stanislaus High School, Mumbai. Thereafter, he attended MMK College, Bandra, Mumbai and the University of Mumbai. He then pursued a doctorate degree in Social Service at Royal American University, United States

Career

Politics

Rrahul Narain Kanal started his political career as the first District President of the Yuva Sena in Mumbai. Yuva Sena is a youth wing of Shiv Sena and is presided by Aditya Uddhav Thackeray. Subsequently, he was elected as the youngest Vice President of the District Yuva Sena community.

Restaurateur

Rrahul Narain Kanal owns Bhaijaanz, a restaurant in Bandra. Salman Khan is his favourite actor, and he has named the restaurant after him.

Controversies

Income Tax Raid

On 8 March 2022, the Income Tax Department conducted a raid at properties associated with Rrahul Kanal. The raid was executed after Rrahul was accused of hawala transactions by Yashwant Jadhav, a leader of Shiv Sena.

Defamation case filed By Rrahul 

In 2022, Rrahul issued a defamation notice to BJP MLA Nitesh Rane over his tweets in which Rane made allegations against Rrahul related to Income Tax searches at RRrahul properties. Nitesh tweeted,

IT raids at Rahul Kanal’s house!! Aur nikalo Humare Aadhish pe morcha..Bola tha.. Waqt Waqt ki baat hai!!..Mobile tower locations and CDR of Kanal on the 8th and 13th night will also help solving the Disha and SSR cases!! Partners in crime?,”

After acknowledging the tweet, Rrahul served a defamation notice to Nitesh Rane. In the notice issued by Rrahul Kanal’s lawyer Shoeb Sheikh, Kunal demanded an apology from Nitesh Rana and further urged him to delete the tweet within 48 hours. Rrahul said in a tweet,

I have further requested my legal team to charge the defamation charges and thereby give a monthly consideration to the respected families who they have gained their Perosnal political… God is Great !!! Satyameva Jayate !!!
