Sofia Wylie

Sofia Wylie, age 16, is an American actress and dancer who is best known for her role in the family series, Andi Mack. She began dancing at 5.

Sofia’s Bio & Family

The 16-years-of-age actress was born as Sofia Wylie on January 7, 2004, in Scottsdale, Arizona, the United States. She is the youngest daughter of her parents, Chris Wylie and Amy Wylie.

Sofia Wylie wishes her elder sister on her birthday on January 3, 2019 (Sofia’s Instagram)

Sofia grew up in Tramonto alongside her older sister Isabella “Bella” Wylie. She was interested in dancing from her young age and later completed her dance training from the Diverse Elements Dance Studio in Phoenix, Arizona. As of her wiki details, Sofia holds white ethnicity and her birth sign is Capricorn.

Career & Net Worth

The talented diva commenced her dance career in 2011 with the reality show, So You Think You Can Dance. She later appeared in the season of America’s Got Talent in 2015. Likewise, Sofia ringed her TV series debut with the 2017 comedy series, Nicky, Ricky, Dicky & Dawn.

Sofia Wylie with Joshua Rush on Andi Mack (IMDB)

Sofia is best known for her part as Buffy Driscoll in the family comedy-drama series, Andi Mack, from 2017 to 2019. Besides, her other major roles include Marvel Rising: Heart of Iron (2019), and High School Musical: The Musical: The Series (2019- present).
