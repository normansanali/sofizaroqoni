Drew Swinney - Age, Family, Bio

American football player who is best known for being a member of the Clemson Tigers football team. He is the son of famous Clemson Tigers football coach Dabo Swinney.

Before Fame

He was raised in a very devoutly religious household.

Trivia

He has amassed over 20,000 followers to his popular Instagram page. He enjoys golfing in his free time. He plays wide receiver for Clemson's football team.

Family Life

He has two brothers, Will and Clay.

Associated With

He has been a member of the Clemson Football program just like famous past player Deshaun Watson.
