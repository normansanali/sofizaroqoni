Rumer Willis News - Us Weekly

Name:Rumer Willis

Hometown:Los Angeles, CA

Birthday:August 16, 1998

About

The eldest daughter of actors Bruce Willis and Demi Moore, Rumer Willis was born August 16, 1988.

Though she was raised in Hailey, Idaho, Willis made her feature film debut in her mother’s 1995 movie Now and Then. She also appeared alongside her mom in 1996’s Striptease and her father in 2000’s The Whole Nine Yards and 2005’s Hostage.

Willis found more big screen success on her own with The House Bunny and Sorority Row. She later landed a recurring role as a teenage lesbian on The CW’s 90210.
