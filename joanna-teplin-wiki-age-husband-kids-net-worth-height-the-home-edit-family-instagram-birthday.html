Joanna Teplin Biography – Joanna Teplin Wiki

Joanna Teplin is the Co-Founder of The Home Edit. She co-founded The Home Edit with Clea Shearer in 2015, four hours after meeting in Nashville. They grew the business solely through social media, attracting the interests of A-list names such as Gwyneth Paltrow, Mandy Moore, Christina Applegate and Thomas Rhett. According to The Home Edit’s website, “The Home Edit organizes every space in the home, from bedrooms and kitchens, to closets and pantries. Every project receives meticulous attention to detail, carefully considered systems, and our signature stylized aesthetic.”

Joanna Teplin and Clea Shearer host Netflix’s home improvement series Get Organized With The Home Edit. The Netflix series follows the business partnership of Clea Shearer and Joanna Teplin as they edit and organize the homes of celebrities and civilians with color-coding, acrylic storage, and handwritten labels.

Get Organized With The Home Edit is executive produced by Reese Witherspoon. Witherspoon previously worked with Clea and Joanna to produce a similar show in 2018 called Master the Mess. The series only lasted one season.

In 2019, Clea Shearer and Teplin published the New York Times best-selling organizing book The Home Edit and their second book, The Home Edit Life: The No-Guilt Guide to Owning What You Want and Organizing Everything, comes out next week.

Teplin graduated in 2001 from the University of California, Santa Barbara, with a degree in film. After graduating, she moved to San Francisco. Her love of cards, stationery and invitations blossomed when she began working at and managing San Francisco’s famous Union Street Papery. When she was 22 years old, she developed a boutique greeting card line, he said. she said.

About 6 years later, Teplin moved with her husband to Los Angeles. In Los Angeles, she expanded her passion for paper, people, and managing the stress of wedding planning by founding Joanna Teplin Invitation Consulting in 2007. After working and living in Los Angeles for two years, her husband’s job transferred them back to San Francisco. In 2011 they had a baby… which led to a new life, new ideas…. and ultimately a new business…. joanna and company- saving time for busy people.

In December 2013, Teplin and her family relocated to the Nashville area for Jeremy’s job. In August 2015, Joanna and Clea Shearer were introduced by a mutual friend and they founded a new home organizing company, The Home Edit. The Home Edit is a full-service home organization company blending functionality with our signature aesthetic.
