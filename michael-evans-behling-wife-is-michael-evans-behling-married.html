Michael Evans Behling wife: Is Michael Evans Behling married?

Michael Evans Behling, an American actor, was born on March 5, 1996. He became most well-known for his role as Jordan Baker on All American.

Behling began modeling while still a student, and his group eventually convinced him to audition for acting roles. He took part in a variety of commercial campaigns for brands like White Castle and Adidas.

Behling made his television debut in the 2017 season of Empire in the role of “Handsome Dude.” In March 2018, he was selected for the part of Jordan Baker on All American, and the pilot’s filming began two weeks later. Behling moved to Los Angeles when filming began in January 2018, and when money was tight, he began sleeping on a friend’s floor.

He made his breakthrough as Jordan Baker. He is currently represented by HRI Talent and Management 101.

Behling and three of his high school friends established the clothing company DesignedAt5AM (Nicholas Stevens, Drew Thompson, and Floyd Athaide). The early morning swim practices of his high school friends and his desire to start each day early in order to accomplish his goals served as the inspiration for the clothing line.
