Vanessa Minnillo, Nick Lachey Celebrate at Their Baby Shower: Picture

Take two! As Vanessa and Nick Lachey anxiously await the birth of their second child, the couple enjoyed a festive baby shower on Saturday, Nov. 1. The parents of 2-year-old Camden posed for a sweet shot at the event, both cradling Vanessa's growing bump. 

"Today was a good day! The perfect 'sprinkle' of love," the former TRL host, 33, captioned the Instagram photo. 

Wearing a black and white printed Rachel Pally long sleeve dress, Nick Lachey's wife looked radiant as her proud hubby held her from behind. In the background of their picture was a table filled with cupcakes and a cake with pink frosting. 

The pair revealed they were having a little girl back in July when the 98 Degrees singer, 40, posted a photo on the beach where he wrote in the sand, "It's a girl." 

Though their little one is surely set to come soon, the Lacheys still celebrated Halloween in style on Friday. Vanessa went all out as Captain Hook to Nick's Peter Pan, while Camden went as the crocodile. 

"The Lacheys in Neverland! HAPPY HALLOWEEN!!!" the proud mom captioned the photo of her family.
