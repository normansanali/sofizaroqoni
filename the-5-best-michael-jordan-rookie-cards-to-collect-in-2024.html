The 5 best Michael Jordan Rookie Cards to Collect in 2024

Michael Jordan, widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of all time, has left an indelible mark on the sports memorabilia market with his iconic rookie cards.

As the demand for basketball cards continues to soar, here's a detailed analysis of the five best Michael Jordan rookie cards to collect in 2024, including their rarity, historical significance, current market value, and notable features.

1. 1986 Fleer Michael Jordan Rookie Card

Rarity and Historical Significance

The 1986 Fleer Michael Jordan rookie card is a highly sought-after collectible due to its historical significance as Jordan's first recognized NBA trading card.

This card is widely considered the cornerstone of modern basketball card collecting and is emblematic of Jordan's legendary career.

Current Market Value

In January 2021, a PSA 10 graded copy of this card sold for an astounding $738,000, setting a new record for the most expensive basketball card ever sold at auction.

2. 1984 Star Michael Jordan #101

Rarity and Historical Significance

The Michael Jordan Star XRC #101 is an iconic trading card featuring Jordan's first-ever NBA appearance, making it a significant piece of basketball history.

With only 3,000 copies reportedly produced, this card's rarity adds to its allure among collectors.

Current Market Value

With a price range of $5,570 to $444,000, a PSA 9 Michael Jordan Star XRC #101 sold for $444,000 in September 2022, further emphasizing its high value and desirability.

3. 1984-85 Star Company Michael Jordan Card

Rarity and Historical Significance

The 1984-85 Star Company Michael Jordan card is considered his true rookie card and is extremely rare, standing as the first official trading card featuring him as a professional basketball player.

Current Market Value

This card has been known to command prices ranging from thousands of dollars to multiple six figures, with a PSA 9 example selling for $444,000, highlighting its immense value and desirability.

4. 1985 Michael Jordan Nike Promo Card

Rarity and Historical Significance

The Michael Jordan Nike Promo card is a highly valued collectible, featuring an iconic image of Jordan dunking in front of the Chicago skyline.

Its association with the promotion of the renowned Air Jordan sneakers adds to its historical significance.

Current Market Value

With a price range of $306 to $12,000 and a PSA 10 supply of 171, this card remains a highly sought-after item, with the potential for significant appreciation in its market worth over time.

5. 1997 Precious Metal Gems Michael Jordan Card

Rarity and Historical Significance

The 1997 Precious Metal Gems Michael Jordan card is an extremely rare limited edition collectible, with only 10 copies in existence, making it an exceptionally coveted item for basketball card enthusiasts.

Current Market Value

This card was sold for a record $350,100 on eBay in 2019, underscoring the immense value and desirability of this rare gem in the sports memorabilia market.

On the whole

These five Michael Jordan rookie cards represent not only a piece of basketball history but also a testament to Jordan's enduring legacy in the sport.

Their rarity, historical significance, and staggering market values make them highly coveted items for collectors and investors alike.
