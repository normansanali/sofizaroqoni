Acting Career & TV Shows

After graduation, he began his acting career in the year 2012. He debuted with the movie named Grave Encounters 2 (2012). Before that, he was an NHL player.

He rose to fame with the role Reilly in the film named Letterkenny, which was released in the year 2016. It is a Canadian based television sitcom. It was formed by Jared Keeso and directed by Jacob Tierney as well as Spencer Maybee.

Moreover, the series was developed and written mainly by Keeso and Jacob Tierney. The show was first started as a YouTube web series with the name Letterkenny Problems, but later it was decided to make it as a television series by Crave in March 2015.

It was first released in February 2016, and later also aired on The Comedy Network. The show follows the residents of Letterkenny, a minor rural community in Ontario roughly based on Keeso’s hometown of Listowel, Ontario.
