Darby Allin Aims to Be The Face of AEW After Mount Everest Conquest

Darby Allin is currently one of the top stars in AEW, who has made a name for himself as a thrill seeker and extreme daredevil. That is not a gimmick as he is exactly like that in real life, which is why he plans on climbing Mount Everest this year. After conquering Mount Everest, Allin has revealed plans to become the face of AEW.

Darby Allin also loves skateboarding and doing a lot of death-defying activities that truly make him feel alive. The former TNT Champion truly knows no fear and has a life-changing plan for this year.

As previously reported, Darby Allin plans on climbing Mount Everest next year. Naturally, this is a major endeavor and there are several risks involved, as Mount Everest is a very dangerous place.

While speaking with Denise Salcedo, Darby Allin revealed that he plans on becoming the face of AEW by winning the AEW World Championship after conquering Mount Everest.

“So to be the face of a company, I believe you have to be World Champion, so that is the next logical step when I get back from Everest because Everest is almost like a vision quest in a way,” Allin said. “I’m going to go dig deep down inside the mountains and find out what it’s really like inside of here.”

Sting and Darby Allin became the new AEW Tag Team Champions last week on AEW Dynamite as well. Nonetheless, only time will tell if Allin will become an AEW World Champion as well.
