Biography

Don Keefer was born in Pennsylvania on August 18, 1916. Remembered for his recurring role as George on the early 1960s CBS sitcom Angel, Keefer is also notable for his numerous guest spots on the western series Gunsmoke. He appeared with Rod Cameron in an episode of the television series State Trooper. He made his cinematic debut in the 1951 movie Death of a Salesman and also appeared early in his career in episodes of the television series Appointment with Adventure and Richard Diamond, Private Detective.
