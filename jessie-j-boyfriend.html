Here’s all you need to know about Jessie J’s boyfriend Chanan Safir Colman (profession, age, and more)

Jessie’s partner Israeli-Danish professional basketball player Chanan Safir Colman competes in the Israel Basketball Premier League. He represents the Hapoel Haifa basketball team, an Israeli organisation with its headquarters in Haifa.

Chanan was born to an Israeli mother and an American Jewish father on March 10, 1984, in Gladsaxe, Denmark. He is 38 years old right now. At the age of just 9, he began playing basketball.

When did Chanan start playing basketball professionally?

Chanan formerly participated in collegiate basketball at Chipola in Florida. In 2004, he began his professional career with the Lappeenrannan NMKY squad in Finland. Soon after, he played for other teams like Maccabi Haifa and Hapoel Jerusalem in Israel as well as Ironi Ashkelon, Svendborg Rabbits, and Randers Cimbria.

The 2006 Finnish League Finals MVP (most valuable player) was Safir Colman. In addition, he received the 2012 Danish League MVP award. In March 2021, he inked a contract with his current squad, Hapeol Hafia. He spent the two years before that playing for the Danish team BMS Herlev Wolfpack.
