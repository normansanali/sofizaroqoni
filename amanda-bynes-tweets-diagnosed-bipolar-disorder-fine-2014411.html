Amanda Bynes Tweets She Was Diagnosed With Bipolar Disorder, Is &quot;Fine&quot;

UPDATE: Later in the morning on Nov. 4, Amanda Bynes tweeted, "I was at a friends apartment last night and one of my friends tweeted my phone , sorry guys !" It is unclear if this tweet is referring to her previous tweets about being diagnosed with bipolar disorder or if she is referring to other tweets from Monday night that have since been deleted.

Original story: For the first time since being released from her treatment facility in Pasadena, Calif. last week, Amanda Bynes has spoken out. The former Nickelodeon star, 28, tweeted a series of apparent explanations on her official page in the early morning hours of Tuesday, Nov. 4. 

“I was diagnosed bi – polar and manic depressive so I'm on medication and I'm seeing my psychologist and pyshchiatrist weekly so I'm fine :D,” she wrote. 

And though she claims to have sorted out her medical issues, Bynes still appears to be struggling with her parents and current conservators, Rick Bynes and Lynn Organ.
