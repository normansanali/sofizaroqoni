Aliyah Boston height: How tall is Aliyah Boston?

In college basketball, Aliyah Boston plays center and power forward for the South Carolina Gamecocks. She excelled in basketball while attending Worcester Academy in Worcester, Massachusetts, winning numerous awards.

Saint Thomas in the U.S. Virgin Islands is where she was born. Boston has also represented the United States in competition and has taken home gold medals in a number of events.

Boston, who was named the NCAA Tournament’s Most Outstanding Player in 2022, assisted South Carolina in capturing their second national title (MOP).

She also received awards for Player and Defensive Player of the Year. Boston was also the three-time winner of the Lisa Leslie Award for best center in NCAA women’s basketball.

Boston developed a passion for basketball at the age of 9 after watching her older sister play. At the age of 12, she and her sister moved to New England to live with their aunt and cousin.

Boston went to Worcester School, where she three times won the Gatorade Massachusetts Player of the Year award and helped her team win the NEPSAC Class A championship in 2019.

Aliyah Boston height: How tall is Aliyah Boston?

Aliyah Boston stands 6 ft 5 in (1.96 m) tall. She participated in the 2019 McDonald’s All-American Game and Jordan Brand Classic and was named an undisputed All-American.
