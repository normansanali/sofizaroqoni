WWE Already Selling Damian Priests Money In The Bank Briefcase For Big Price Tag

WWE knows how to make money, and their merchandise machine is at it again. Damian Priest got new Money in the Bank briefcase during RAW, and it didn’t take WWE very much time to get their shop involved.

Click here for our complete coverage of WWE RAW this week.

JD McDonagh came out to present Damian Priest with his new purple Money in the Bank briefcase. The new design is purple, and has “SENOR” on it, like a spray-painted stencil. Priest really liked the gift as well.

WWE already has replica of Damian Priest’s Money in the Bank briefcase. That new purple look also comes with an increased price tag.

The new Money in the Bank briefcase is 100 bucks, which is a lot more than they used to be. They marked those briefcases up for live events, but they could typically be found on the WWE shop for far less.

We will have to see how many of those purple Money in the Bank briefcases we see pop up in the future. It might be a nice bit of merchandise to have, and it also functions as an actual briefcase, but that $100 price tag is a bit steep.

We will have to see how long Damian Priest holds that Money in the Bank briefcase. Now that WWE is marketing it to sell, chances are they will make the most of the situation.
