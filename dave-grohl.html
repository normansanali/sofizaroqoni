Dave Grohl Net Worth, Age, Girlfriend, Family, Biography &amp; More

Dave Grohl or David Eric Grohl is an American who is a popular American musician by profession. He first gained prominence as the drummer for the grunge band Nirvana, and later as the band’s founder, primary songwriter, guitarist, and lead singer.

He is also the drummer and co-founder of the rock band Them Crooked Vultures, as well as a member of the short-lived Late! and Probot side projects. Grohl has also collaborated with Queens of the Stone Age on recordings and tours.

Dave Grohl Wiki/Biography

Born on 14 January 1969, Dave Grohl’s age is 53 years as of 2022. He was born and raised in a well-settled Christian family from Warren, Ohio, United States. He is an American by nationality and has his belief in the Christianity religion.

He began his early schooling at Bishop Ireton High School in Alexandria, United States, and later took admission in Annandale High School, United States but he dropped out of the school to pursue his career in music.

After that, he enrolled himself at a band named Scream by giving an audition to it. Later on, he joined and created many other music bands. From the very beginning of his childhood, he was more interested in singing and music rather than in studying.
