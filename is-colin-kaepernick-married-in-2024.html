Public Declarations of Love

Colin Kaepernick has publicly declared his love and appreciation for Nessa Diab on social media. Nessa, in turn, has written about how Colin inspires her, emphasizing their love and mutual support for each other.

Marriage Status

As of 2024, there is no public information to suggest that Colin Kaepernick and Nessa Diab are married. They have been in a committed relationship for several years and are devoted parents to their child, but they have not publicly announced any plans for marriage.
