Kim Scott Mathers and Eminem’s Rocky Relationship

Despite the tumultuous times, Eminem and Kim remarried in 2006, but their second attempt at marriage was short-lived as they divorced just a few months later.

Kim Scott Mathers Run-Ins With The Law

Despite these difficulties, Kim has worked hard to overcome them and rebuild her life.
