Jimmy Garoppolo Net Worth 2024

Jimmy Garoppolo is an NFL quarterback who was born on November 2, 1991 in Arlington Heights, IL. He was drafted in the second round of the 2014 NFL Draft by the New England Patriots after a successful collegiate career with the Eastern Illinois Panthers. He won two Super Bowls with the Patriots, serving as a backup to Tom Brady. In 2017, he was traded to the San Francisco 49ers.

About

NFL quarterback selected in the second round of the 2014 NFL Draft by the New England Patriots after a standout collegiate career with the Eastern Illinois Panthers. He won Super Bowls XLIX and LI with the Patriots, serving as a backup to Tom Brady. In 2017, he was traded to the San Francisco 49ers.

Trivia
