Louis Partridge On Netflix's 'Enola Holmes'

Netflix dropped its most anticipated movie, Enola Holmes, on 23 September 2020 on its platform. The film tells Sherlock Holmes' sister, Enola Holmes, which surrounds her adventurous journey in finding her missing mom.

The movie is based on the novel by Nancy Springer, which stars the artists including Henry Cavill, Sam Clafin, Helena Bonham Carter, Millie Bobby Brown, Louis Partridge, and many others.

(©: IMDB)

See more about the young and aspiring British actor, Louis Partridge, below.

Who Is Louis Partridge?

Louis Partridge, age 17, is an aspiring British actor who portrays Lord Tewkesbury, Enola's love interest in the recently released movie, Enola Holmes, alongside Millie Bobby Brown.

Regarding his role as Tewkesbury in the film, Partridge mentioned, 'There's a bit of me in that character… probably a bit too much. It was brilliant to properly sit down and try to take a character off the page, as that wasn't something I had done before. We brought warmth to him.'

He is a promising Instagram star, who has 392k followers on his official Instagram account, as of now.

Louis Partridge was born in 2003 in Wandsworth, London, England, and his birthday is on 3 June. Concerning his physique, he measures at a decent height of 6 feet tall (1.83 meters). 

Partridge recently finished his GSCE exam. He said to Vogue, 'I was straight into filming once the exams were done. The whole audition process happened when I was revising, and I was very much more into focusing on the auditions than the exams.'

Louis Partridge's Career And Net Worth

Enola Holmes (2020) might be the notable movie of Louis Partridge, but it is not the first movie of the young actor. He is around the entertainment industry for more than a half-decades since 2014.

Partridge started his acting career with the role of Alf in the 2014 television series Boomers. In the same year, he starred in the short movies Beneath Water and About a Dog.

Louis Partridge alongside Millie Bobby Brown in 'Enola Holmes' (©: Vogue)

The following year, he appeared in Pan; however, he was uncredited for his role in the production. Besides that, he has appeared in several movies and tv shows, including Amazon Adventure (2017), Paddington 2 (2017), Medici (2019), and many others.
