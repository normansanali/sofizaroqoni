About

Celebrity family member and public figure who is recognized for being the mother of professional football players Travis Kelce and Jason Kelce. She has more than 430,000 followers on her personal donnakelce Instagram account.

In high school, she won the Junior Olympics competition for track and field.

She has been a guest on her sons' New Heights podcast. She wrote both of her son's personal letters before their appearances in Super Bowl LVII. She was in the audience when her son Travis hosted Saturday Night Live.

She was married to Ed Kelce for nearly 25 years. However, the couple would later divorce.

In September of 2023, she was seen with Taylor Swift at a Kansas City Chiefs game. The pop star is rumored to be dating her son Travis.
