Chris Paddock Obituary, Death Cause – The death of Chris Paddock has occurred. Paddock reportedly worked as a teacher, coach, and leader at Fannin Middle School, Travis Middle School, Caprock High School, Palo Duro High School, and Sleepy Hollow Elementary School, according to a post made by the AISD.
On Saturday, it was confirmed by authorities from the Amarillo Independent School District that Chris Paddock, the principal of Sleepy Hollow Elementary, had passed away.

Paddock started his career at AISD in 2001 and was described as an inspirational leader, a helpful mentor, and a friend in a post that was made public on Facebook by AISD.
