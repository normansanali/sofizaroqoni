Yevgeny Prigozhin Wife, Lyubov Prigozhina: All You Need To Know

Lyubov Valentinovna Prigozhina, Yevgeny Prigozhin wife, stands as a notable figure in her own right. 

The news of the private jet crash in August 2023, which included Yevgeny Prigozhin, the head of the Wagner Group, was a tragic event that shocked many. 

His death was felt, not just in Russia but across the world, owing to his role as a Russian oligarch and his connections with Russian President Vladimir Putin. 

Let’s look deeper into the life of his wife, Lyubov Prigozhina, who has remained largely under the radar, despite her association with her husband.

Russian Plane Crash

Yevgeny Prigozhin, the well-known leader of the Wagner Group, is believed to have met a tragic end in a plane crash on August 23rd near Moscow’s Tver region. 

This incident occurred just two months after his mercenary army staged a violent revolt against Russia.

The crash, which claimed the lives of all 10 passengers on board, is said to have been caused by Russian air defenses, as reported by social media channels linked to the Wagner group. 

According to the Grey Zone Telegram channel, Prigozhin’s demise was attributed to “actions of traitors to Russia.” 

Prigozhin was previously involved in an unsuccessful rebellion against the Russian armed forces. 

Speculations persist, both domestically and internationally, that the rebellion was staged, and Prigozhin abandoned his “justice march” to Moscow following direct orders from President Vladimir Putin.

The fatal incident occurred in the Tver region, located northwest of Moscow, coinciding with rumors of Senior Russian General Sergei Surovikin being dismissed from his role as the air force chief. 

It is worth mentioning that Surovikin was known to have amicable relations with Prigozhin and had not been seen publicly since the attempted mutiny. 

The unfortunate incident

The ill-fated aircraft, an Embraer-135 (EBM-135BJ), was en route from Moscow to St Petersburg, carrying a total of seven passengers and three crew members, according to the Russian aviation authority, Rosaviatsia.

Dmitry Utkin, a prominent commander within the Wagner group who established the organization in 2014, was also listed as a passenger on the ill-fated plane. 

Reports indicate that the crash occurred near the village of Kuzhenkino, roughly halfway between Moscow and St Petersburg. 

Local residents reported hearing two explosions prior to the crash, along with witnessing two vapor trails. 

Tass news agency revealed that the aircraft caught fire upon impact with the ground, adding that the duration of the flight was less than 30 minutes.

Authorities have launched an investigation into the incident, while emergency services continue to search the crash site. 

Additionally, the Grey Zone reported that a second business jet owned by Prigozhin had safely landed in the Moscow region. Updates on the situation are available through the provided sources.

The Wagner group, known for its brutal tactics, has an estimated 25,000 fighters and has been involved in various conflicts in Ukraine, Syria, and West Africa. 

Prigozhin orchestrated the mutiny that occurred on June 23-24, mobilizing his troops from Ukraine, seizing the southern Russian city of Rostov-on-Don, and posing a potential threat to Moscow.

Who Was Yevgeny Prigozhin Wife?

Lyubov Prigozhina, born on June 26, 1970, was a Russian businesswoman and pharmacist who was married to Yevgeny Prigozhin. 

She completed her high school education in Russia and pursued higher studies in the medical field, graduating with a degree in pharmacy. 

Her prominence, however, is largely due to her association with her husband.

Lyubov Prigozhina’s Career

Lyubov Prigozhina is the proprietor of a chain of boutique establishments in Saint Petersburg, known as the Chocolate Museum. 

In addition, she owns a wellness center in the Leningrad region, which caters to the community’s health needs. 

She also owns a boutique hotel named the Crystal Spa & Residence, which was awarded the esteemed Perfect Spa Project award in 2013.

Apart from these ventures, she owns Agat LLC, a subsidiary of Concord Management and Consulting LLC, which is part of the Concord group. 

The Concord group is believed to be connected to Yevgeny Prigozhin and is known for its involvement in political and commercial activities.

Due to the benefits her husband gained from public contracts with the Russian Ministry of 

Defense during the Crimea annexation and the pro-Russian separatist occupation in eastern Ukraine, several Western nations imposed financial sanctions on Lyubov Prigozhina. 

Nonetheless, she continues to support her husband significantly and wields considerable influence.

Despite her association with Yevgeny Prigozhin and her prominence in business, Lyubov Prigozhina intentionally keeps a low profile and avoids attracting attention. 

She operates discreetly, managing the Wagner Group’s operations from behind the scenes.
