Capital One CEO Mike Wassmer obituary, burial, funeral, memorial service

Mike Wassmer, born and raised in the United States of America, was known to be the executive president of the renowned Capital One Financial Corp which has its headquarters in Richmond, Central Virginia the United States.

Capital One is a company that specializes in credit cards, auto loans, banking and savings accounts.

Wassmer began with Capital One in July 1994 and has been with the company until he became the president in 2020.

According to sources, he owns more than 2,081 shares of the company which worth about $9.1 million.

Mike Wassmer passed away on June 16, 2022 at age 46. His death was confirmed by an executive of Capital One on his Twitter handle.
