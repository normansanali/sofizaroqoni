Adam Cole Explains His Actions, Forms Undisputed Kingdom On 1/3 AEW Dynamite

Adam Cole has formed The Undisputed Kingdom.

The first AEW Dynamite of 2024 opened with Adam Cole, Matt Taven, Mike Bennett, Roderick Strong, and Wardlow making an in-ring appearance to address their formation against MJF at AEW Worlds End. Cole initially started by saying we shouldn’t be surprised and that MJF is gone and “is never coming back.”

Cole also stated that he lost everything by teaming with MJF, not the other way around. MJF needed Cole, but Cole didn’t need MJF. Cole explained why the other four behind him are his actual friends and noted that each of them are going to hold gold as The Kingdom has the ROH World Tag Team Championship, while Strong will target the International title. His plan also calls for Wardlow to go after the AEW World title so that he will eventually forfeit it to Cole when he’s cleared.

Before Undisputed Kingdom could leave, Jay White interrupted and expressed his displeasure over being used as a catalyst. White didn’t come alone as The Gunns joined him in storming the ring to brawl with Strong and The Kingdom. The Acclaimed and Billy Gunn then ran down to make the save and Undisputed Kingdom retreated.
