The Meaning Behind The Song: Make Me Nervous by Enphamus

TitleArtistWriter/ComposerAlbumRelease DateGenreProducer
Make Me NervousEnphamusMilan Made It, Enphamus & Anti Da MenaceErrybody Hates 90January 12, 2023RapMilan Made It

Enphamus’s song “Make Me Nervous” holds a deeper meaning within its lyrics. With its intense and powerful delivery, the song reflects the artist’s emotions and experiences. The lyrics paint a vivid picture of Enphamus’s outlook on life, showcasing his confidence and determination.

As I listen to the song, I am immediately drawn to the chorus where Enphamus opens with the line, “F-f-fuck you doin’? Buddy listen, please don’t make me nervous.” This line sets the tone for the entire song, expressing Enphamus’s desire for others to acknowledge his presence without causing any uncomfortable feelings. It illustrates his need for respect and recognition in a world that often disregards those who are trying to make a name for themselves.

The lyrics continue to showcase Enphamus’s self-assurance and fearlessness. He proclaims, “I pop it when I want, these niggas pop it when they can,” highlighting his ability to act on his own terms. This declaration of independence reflects his refusal to conform to societal expectations and his determination to pave his own way.

Furthermore, Enphamus’s lyrics also touch on themes of loyalty and betrayal. He warns, “Seen a snake, wait until he seen cheese to kill his man,” suggesting that trust should not be easily given, as people may turn on you when the opportunity arises. This cautionary tale serves as a reminder to be wary of those who may be driven by self-interest and not genuine loyalty.

On a personal level, “Make Me Nervous” resonates with me in a profound way. As an individual striving to pursue my own dreams and passions, I can relate to Enphamus’s desire to be taken seriously and his frustration with those who undermine his ambitions. This song serves as a powerful anthem that reminds me to stay focused, confident, and resilient despite any obstacles or doubts that may come my way.

The collaboration with Anti Da Menace in the second verse adds another layer of intensity to the song. Their contrasting styles complement each other, creating a dynamic and captivating listening experience. Anti Da Menace’s lyrics delve into themes of violence and revenge, further enhancing the raw emotion conveyed by Enphamus.

Overall, “Make Me Nervous” is not just a mere song but rather a statement of Enphamus’s identity and determination. It is a powerful expression of his unwavering self-belief, urging listeners to embrace their own strengths and pursue their goals with urgency.

As Enphamus delivers each line with conviction and confidence, the song becomes an anthem for those who refuse to be silenced or overlooked. It serves as a powerful reminder to stay true to oneself and push forward, even when faced with adversity.

Enphamus’s “Make Me Nervous” is a testament to the artist’s resilience, proving that he is more than just a name but a force to be reckoned with. With its impactful lyrics and dynamic collaboration, the song leaves a lasting impression on listeners, urging them to embrace their own power and stand tall in the face of any challenges that come their way.
