Avril Lavigne's Dating History: Brody Jenner, Mod Sun and More

Credit: Vincent Yu/AP/Shutterstock

Chad Kroeger

Lavigne met the “Rockstar” singer while working on her self-titled album with him in March 2012.

During a 2018 interview with Billboard, the Under My Skin musician recalled her thought process about collaborating with Kroeger at the time.

“He’s had a ton of hit songs. He plays guitar. This could be great,” she explained. “A month later, I had a 14-carat ring on my finger.”

The songwriters wed in July 2013 and announced their separation in September 2015. Three years later, Lavigne was still singing her ex’s praises, telling Billboard, “Chad’s band has sold, like, over 50 million albums! They’re selling out arenas worldwide!”
