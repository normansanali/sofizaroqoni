Who Are Tejumola And Bosun Olubeko?

Meet the two siblings of Dotun Olubeko known as Tejumola and Bosun Olubeko. Dotun signed up for The Bachelorette and makes it to the final four of the 2023 season. How his journey pans out you’ll have to wait and see.

Meanwhile, here we bring details about his siblings. This article covers details about their age, job, marriage, and current residence.

The Bachelorette: Who Are Dotun Olubeko Siblings?

One of the Bachelorette’s final four contestants, according to Reality Steve, is Dotun Olubeko. He and his two siblings Tejumola and Bosun Olubeko were born to their parents Yomi and Adenike Olubeko who were Nigerian immigrants.

Their dad Yomo is age 58. He served in Fresno Sheriff Dept. He held the position of Deputy Sheriff from 1999 to 2017. Yomo announced his retirement after nearly 18 years.

Adenike, the mother, reached age 56 in October 2022 and celebrates her birthday on 15 October. Per Adenike Olubeko’s LinkedIn, she worked as a registered nurse in the State Of California.

Yomo and Adenike had Dotun as the youngest of three children as he is only 30.

Dotun Olubeko is an Integrative Medicine and Functional Health specialist. He worked full-time as a functional health consultant and performance coach, specializing in creating high-quality dietary, fitness, and lifestyle solutions that are “restorative, sustainable, and ultimately designed to optimize performance and empower individuals in their day-to-day.”

This Bachelorette participant also is the founder of DPT Fitness, a program where personalization and evidence-based dietary, fitness, and lifestyle plans meet to optimize human health. Mostly, this program approaches busy professionals and athletes through carefully curated nutrition and exercise plans along with lifestyle recommendations.

Besides DPT, Dotun also offered his services as a Performance Guide at Lifeforce.

Prior to this, Dotun was an Athletics Partnership Director at The BodyBuilding LLC for a year and a freelancing Sports Nutrition Consultant for 2 ½ years.

As for his education, Dotun attended California State University, Chico from 2015 to 2018 for his degrees in “Minor, Health and Wellness” and “Bachelor of Science – BS, Nutrition Sciences.” Then, he joined the University of Western States for his Master of Science in Functional Medicine & Human Nutrition from 2020 to 2022.

During his college days, Dotun was a football and track and field athlete, NCAA All-American, and professional hurdler. He also spent much of his undergrad writing meal plans for his fellow teammates and working in biochemical lab research on campus after practices.

Dotun was one of the 25 men vying for Charity Lawson’s heart on The Bachelorette season 20. According to Reality Steve, he even made it to Charity’s final four alongside Xavier Bonner, Joey Graziadei, and Aaron Bryant.

“I definitely am looking for someone who is a compassionate person, so someone who is extremely kind and treats others well. So, anyone who can come up with a good joke or two is a winner.”
